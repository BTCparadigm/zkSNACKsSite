Nowadays it is all about individuals' digital privacy, governments and organisations are making steps to protect privacy in the digital world. We believe that privacy is both a fundamental human right and business need that should be preserved at all times. This is why we focus on your digital financial privacy.

Mission

zkSNACKs was established to research the possibilities for Bitcoin fungibility enhancements and provide different products ensuring the desired privacy. Many believe that Bitcoin is anonymous but in reality the Blockchain is transparent.

Our mission is to equip you with powerful tools to secure your privacy.

zkSNACKs' flagship product is a privacy oriented Bitcoin wallet, called Wasabi Wallet.
